Discover Hurricane

Welcome to Hurricane. A geographical wonder with a city that has flurished.

Home of the American Legion Equestrian Park. A beautiful place to ride horses and enjoy the outdoors.

Ironman 70.3 St. George. A yearly event that brings athletes from all over the world.

Jellystone Water Park. A fun place for families to enjoy the water and sun.

Peach Days. An annual celebration of the past with events and activities for all ages.

Sandhollow State Park. A beautiful place to enjoy the water and sun.

Historic Canal Trail. There is no life without water. Take a stroll back in time and enjoy the history of Hurricane and how it has "blossomed like a rose."

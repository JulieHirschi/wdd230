Project Milestone - The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name:

Hurricane Chamber of Commerce. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose:

The Hurricane Chamber of Commerce helps highlight local business and activites in an effort to sustain and improve economic growth. The website will act as a source for visitors to know what the city has to offer to both community members and tourists.We support our members by providing opportunities for growth and success. Through connections made with each other and the community by networking, advocacy, and events.
